Meet Our Instructors

Sarah Johnson

Sarah is an experienced IELTS coach with over 10 years of experience helping students achieve their desired band scores. She specializes in creating personalized study plans, providing in-depth feedback, and sharing practical strategies to improve speaking, writing, listening, and reading skills. Sarah’s patient and friendly approach makes her a favorite among our students.

Mark Lee

Mark brings a wealth of experience in teaching English for Business Communication. With a background in corporate training and over 15 years in the field, Mark helps professionals enhance their English fluency and communication skills in business settings. His sessions cover real-world scenarios, from presentations to negotiations, to boost confidence and success.

Emily Thompson

Emily is passionate about making English learning fun and engaging for children. She has 8 years of experience working with young learners and uses interactive methods like storytelling, games, and songs to keep classes lively. Emily’s warm personality and creative teaching style help children build a strong foundation in English with ease.

Daniel White

Daniel is a seasoned Academic English Writing instructor who has guided countless students in mastering essay and research writing for university success. His clear explanations and structured approach to academic writing help learners develop strong arguments, improve grammar, and refine their writing style for better results in exams and assignments.
